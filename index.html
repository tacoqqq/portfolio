I am an aspiring, ever-growing web developer who is currently based in the Greater Los Angeles Area. To answer Marie Kondo’s question on what sparks my joy: a cup of coffee in the morning, fulfilling fitness training, and developing user-friendly and modern-designed websites that help solve people’s problems.
